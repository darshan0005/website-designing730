what is seo ? seo : seo stands for search engine optimization seo is a strategic approach to increasing the visibility of a website or web page on search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. It involves optimizing the website's content, structure, and metadata for better search engine rankings and user experience. Here are some key steps in seo: 1. Conduct keyword research: Identify the most relevant keywords and phrases that users are searching for on your website. This will help you tailor your content and improve your website's search engine visibility. 2. Create high-quality content: Write engaging, informative, and relevant content that resonates with your target audience. Use SEO-friendly titles, meta descriptions, and rich content (such as images and videos) to improve search engine visibility. 3. Optimize your website's structure: Structure your website hierarchically and organize your content in a logical and easy-to-navigate manner. Use meta tags, internal links, and external links to improve website rankings.
